About the Workshop

As part of ICSR 2026 + Art — the global forum exploring the intersection of social robotics and creative practice — this two-hour workshop hosted by Innovate UK brings together academics, government representatives, industry leaders, and innovators.

Together, participants explore how robotics, advanced and novel materials, and artistic practice can converge to shape future UK creative and industrial capabilities. The session provides a live policy feedback loop: insights generated feed directly into Innovate UK's mission to accelerate innovation and strengthen national capabilities.

By combining creative robotics, material innovation, and interdisciplinary dialogue, this session aims to uncover new pathways for the UK's leadership at the intersection of art, engineering, and technology.
